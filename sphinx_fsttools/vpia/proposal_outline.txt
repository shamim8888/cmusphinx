System Concept
--------------

Voice-driven personal wiki:

 * Tool for organizing work and ideas
 * Simple document structure
 * Automatic linking
 * Automatic classification of incoming utterances

Personal knowledge manager:

 * Quick notes capability - todo lists, factoids
 * Query system for notes and conversations
 * Summarization of pages
 * Interactive voice editing of notes and factoids

Refinements beyond a dictation system:

 * Structured information (pages, links)
 * Automatic factoid extraction and classification
 * Interactive speech query interface

Data Organization
-----------------

Pages (a.k.a. Topics)
Factoids (a.k.a. Notes)
Utterances
Sub-utterance structure
